Executive Summary

White Car generated 19,836 total platform conversions at a blended CPA of SAR 7.07, with TikTok driving the majority of scale. TikTok represents around 91% of spend and 88% of conversions. TikTok is carrying most of the spend and conversions. This drives scale but creates concentration risk.

The most efficient platform by reported CPA is Google (SAR 4.38), but its spend volume is much smaller, so performance should be validated by lead quality before major budget shifts. The next priority is to validate lead quality, connect ad conversions to CRM outcomes, and restructure campaign naming, tracking, and budget allocation.

Platform CPA vs Real CPA

Platform CPA is based on ad-reported conversions. For a car business, the real metric should be cost per qualified lead and cost per sale. Below is the recommended lead lifecycle to track.

New LeadForm / call / WhatsApp
Valid LeadReal contact, correct city
Qualified LeadHas need, has car/budget
Sales AcceptedContacted & accepted
AppointmentShowroom / test drive
Closed DealRevenue

Example: TikTok may show SAR 7.35 CPA, but if only 10% of leads are qualified, the real qualified-lead cost is ~SAR 73.50. Google may show SAR 4.38 CPA, but if intent is stronger, it may be far more valuable despite lower spend.

Strategic Recommendations

1. Do not judge performance by CPA alone

TikTok drives the majority of leads, but the business must validate which platform produces qualified leads and sales opportunities.

2. Reduce TikTok waste before scaling

Campaigns above the blended CPA should be reviewed, especially high-spend campaigns with CPA above SAR 9–13.

3. Relaunch Meta with a cleaner structure

Meta shows strong CPA potential, but the current setup appears paused or inconsistent. Relaunch with separated audiences, clear naming, and creative testing.

4. Give Google a controlled scale test

Google has the lowest CPA, but spend is very small. Increase gradually only after reviewing search terms and lead quality.

5. Add CRM outcome tracking

The report should show not only leads, but qualified leads, sales calls, appointments, visits, and closed deals.

6. Build a creative testing system

TikTok and Meta need weekly creative testing by hook, car model, offer, and audience segment.
